Rob Nelson About Me

Inspired Software Developer skilled in software develoment, team leadership, customer interaction, and system deployment. Works best when faced with ambiguous requirements and tasked to develop a solution through working with clients and end-users. Developed and deployed an analytics client supporting a large data collection system. Extensive background in database development, data loading, architecture and design, Python, Java, C/C++, Linux, and team building.

I've spent a good deal of my free time the last year studying HTML/CSS/JS, the MERN stack and the technologies that support development of modern applications and architectures. My objective isn't simply to learn about the technologies; it's to expand my skill set and follow my passion for developing useful products for end users. There is a great deal of satisfaction to creating tools people use to address critical problems. The satisfaction is greater when the tool is up front and not hidden in the background. Creating the UI also provides opportunities to work collaboratively with the end users. My ultimate goal is to land another position leading a team in developing or growing a Web-based tool that benefits the public.
